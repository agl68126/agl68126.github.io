Georgetown Cupcake



Georgetown Cupcake is a light-filled corner space specializes in gourmet cupcakes made on-site daily. One of the most popular places to go get a cupcake. They are best known for their red velvet.



National Zoo



The National Zoo is home to 2,000 individual animals of nearly 400 different species. They are best known for the Pandas, Tian Tian, Mei Xiang, and Bao Bao. The Zoo is a place where people can marvel at wonderful animals, learn about wildlife and its conservation, and enjoy a beautiful, peaceful experience among our gardens.



Basilica of the National Shrine



The Basilica of the National Shrine of the Immaculate Conception honors the Blessed Virgin Mary as the Immaculate Conception. The shrine is also the largest Catholic Church in the United States and North America, one of the ten largest churches in the world.
